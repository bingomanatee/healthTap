What is an "active" project?

Active projects are the projects you are currently working on. If you’re done working on a project, you can archive it and it won’t be counted against your total. Technically you could have hundreds of archived projects and still fit on the $25/mo plan as long as you have no more than 8 active projects.

Does everyone on my team need to subscribe?

No! You only need to subscribe with one account. This account can create the projects, invite others and assign admins to manage the projects as necessary. All the invited team members can use Zeplin with their individual accounts and upload designs.
